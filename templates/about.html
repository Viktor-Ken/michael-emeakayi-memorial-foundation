About Us

Honouring the legacy of High Chief Michael Emeakayi by empowering the next generation through education, agriculture and digital skills.

The Foundation’s Origin

The Michael Emeakayi Memorial Foundation is a registered non-profit established in loving memory of High Chief Michael Emeakayi of Umuohi, Okija. Founded in April 2025 by his children, the Foundation continues his legacy by creating opportunities for young people and supporting underserved families through targeted and sustainable programmes.

Our Mission

To deliver transformative, community-led initiatives that promote academic excellence, agricultural innovation, and digital empowerment for young people and families in underserved communities.

Our Vision

To build a self-reliant, educated, and empowered society through integrated grassroots programmes in education, agriculture, and digital skills.

Who We Are

The Foundation serves as the umbrella for a growing portfolio of developmental programmes. We focus on youth empowerment, education, sustainable agriculture and digital literacy across Umuohi and beyond.

The Legacy of High Chief Michael Emeakayi

High Chief Michael Emeakayi was known for his generosity and commitment to education and agriculture. His values continue through the Foundation — honouring his life by uplifting future generations.
